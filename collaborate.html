Collaborate

We are open to local and foreign collaborations that advance people, innovation, and technology for public benefit.

PIANDT welcomes partners who share our commitment to useful, ethical, and accessible technology. Whether you are nearby or across borders, we are ready to explore joint research, co-development, education, consultancy, and community programmes.

Tell us who you are, what you hope to build together, and how we can begin. Every serious enquiry is reviewed with care.

Local partnerships

We seek collaboration with universities, labs, charities, SMEs, public bodies, and community organisations in our region—shared facilities, joint pilots, student projects, and applied research that serves people where they live.

International partnerships

We welcome foreign institutions, research consortia, diasporic networks, and industry partners for cross-border programmes, knowledge exchange, dual-site experiments, and technology transfer that respects local needs and global standards.
